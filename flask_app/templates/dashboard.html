About Us and where we go...

Florida Disaster Rescue was founded in 2018 by a group of volunteers that wanted to help others in need. We have responded to many hurricanes including Irma, Michael, Sally, Laura, Dorian to Ian (2022) and the eathquake in Haiti as well as the structure collapse in Miami in 2021.

We have been working all around the world helping countries repair themselves after natural disasters like Puerto Rico. We are dedicated to helping others 24 hours a day / 7 days a week whether we are getting trapped families out of a flooded home or whether it is cooking food and doing local food drives to those affected in anyway. Florida Disaster Rescue's team is comprised of highly motivated individuals who put the needs of others above all else, when a disaster occurs they are some of the first people to jump into action. They are committed to training and preparing constantly. When Florida Disaster Rescue is not out in the field, they are working in the background to help coordinate supplies and recovery efforts. We hope we are never needed in your community, however if in the event we are, you can reach us here rest assured you will have some of the best and hardest working individuals to help you along.
